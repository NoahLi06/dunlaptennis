ACES FOR ARIAN

2023 Aces For Arian Tennis Tournament
July 1st & 2nd

The Aces For Arian Tournament will be held again in 2023, in honor of former Dunlap Eagle and USC Trojan Arian Rahbar.
Arian passed away due to senseless street racing in December of 2021. Rahbar was a member of the Winning 2017 Dunlap Tennis team and qualified
for state two times after. On the court, he was known for his quick speed and punishing forehands. Off the court, he was a phenomenal and intelligent person.

Arian's fighting spirit with live on as we gather to play the sport he loved.

Registration will be coming soon!
